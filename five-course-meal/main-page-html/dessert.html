Dessert

Dessert is the last course of a meal.

In the United States, dessert is likely to consist of pastry, cake, ice cream, pudding, or fresh or cooked fruit. British meals traditionally end with nuts, fruits, and port or other dessert wine, while French practice is to end with fruit, cheese, and wine, in both Britain and France, a more elaborate meal would include a sweet course preceding the dessert offerings.
